Minecraft Luneta Park

Luneta Park is a recreational landscape project that I designed to create an accessible and inviting public space for leisure, movement, and community interaction. The design integrates organized pedestrian circulation, landscaped green spaces, and shaded seating areas to establish a balanced and functional environment for visitors.

Institutional • West Virginia, USA

Allegheny Lunatic Asylum of 1864

I designed the Trans‑Allegheny Lunatic Asylum of 1864 to combine structured, therapeutic spaces with architectural grandeur.
Its radial wings and expansive corridors promote light, ventilation, and order, creating a sense of calm while subtly reflecting the imposing, somber reality of confinement.

Urban Furniture • Cebu, Philippines

Triple Arm Anti-Homeless Bench

I designed the Vigilant Urban Bench to champion daytime relaxation while preventing nighttime lounging. The strategically placed central armrest ensures that no one can stretch out for a nap, keeping the bench upright, tidy, and socially “responsible.”
